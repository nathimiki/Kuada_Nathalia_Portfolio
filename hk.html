The Project

College Assignment: Design and Image

For this project, we were required to create a logo and presentation focusing in our parents and it should be something related to design. The brand identity should represent/personify/identify/evoke the characteristics or essence of our parent.

The Result

The type of design chosen was a Interior Design Company. I created the company thinking about my father's personality and hobbies, for being a creative and perfectionist person, also because he is always building and refurbishing our house. The idea was to keep the logo simple with pure typography, but at same time give it a high-quality impression.
