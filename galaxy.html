Glalaxy Exposion

Branding | Web Design

The Project

College Assignment: Eletronic Web Development

For this project, we were required to create a website based on a fictional product or company. I create the branding, identity and website of a company called Galaxy Explosion, a premium brand of vape eliquids/juice.

The Result

It was the most enjoyable assignment that I had so far. I decided to create a eliquid/juice brand, because it's something very present in my day, so I had some repertory. The idea was to make a black and white website, to keep it clean and simple to evoke a premium feeling. In order to balance this, I decided to create a colorfull logo and label design to highlight on the website and on the shelf. The name Galaxy Explosion was chosen inspired in the 4 flavors Mars, Jupiter, Uranus and Venus.
