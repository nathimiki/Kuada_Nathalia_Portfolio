Chips Ahoy Beer

Branding | identity

The Project

College Assignment: Eletronic Image Production

For this project, each student received a famous brand, in my case it was Chips Ahoy. The task was to design a label for a beer or wine bottle in the brand style.

The Result

The biggest challenge of this assignment was to keep the Chips Ahoy brand identity. The idea was to create a label to give a impression of a Craft Beer, in order to archive that I chose a handwritten typography and some wheat elements. The chosen colours were meant to transmit the fun/happy essence of the brand.
