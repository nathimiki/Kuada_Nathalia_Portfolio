AquaPomus

Branding | 3D Design

The Project

College Assignment: Eletronic Image Production and 3D Animation

For this project, each group was required to create an own unique design and functionality for a bottle with detachable infuser pod. We were required to develop each stage of this product, from concept to finished 3D model. For this project we used Illustrator, Photoshop, Cinema 4D and AfterEffects.

The Brand

The Aqua Pomus Fruit Infuser Bottle is the easiest way to always stay hydrated. This amazing water bottle with infusor for fruits, vegetables or herbs will naturally make you ingest more fluids and keep yourself hydrated throughout the day. Proper hydration helps you nourish and oxygenate your muscles, have more energy, and look healthier and healthier looking. Light and easy to carry, this bottle is perfect for the gym, work, school or even for travel. And you'll still save good money by stopping you from spending horrors with sodas, juices or other drinks that are filled with sugar. And on top of everything, you still help the environment by stopping consuming and throwing plastic bottles out. And it's very easy to use by simply filling the infuser with your favorite fruit, or even sliced vegetables or herbs, wait 5-10 minutes for the flavor to spread.

The Name and Logo

We wanted a fun a innovative name for the bottle, so we decided to play with languages and ended up with "AquaPomus", which means "WaterFruit" in Latin. The logo is constituted only of typography. As the main focus is the typography’s shape, the logo aims to bring some informality and playfullness to the brand. We also used drawings of fruits for the package to complement the design. We decided on the colors with the objective of making the brand “lighter”, more approachable and more lively.

The 3D

We designed the bottle from scratch. We made a sketch in paper, transformed it in a vector at Illustrator, then designed each part of the bottle at Cinema 4D. The last process was to place the logo and writing, animate the bottle and create the video.

The Result

The biggest challenge of this assignment was to designed the bottle from scratch. It was an extensive research process, because we had to understand the purpose of each part of a infuser bottle to create the 3D model.

Let’s break the limits of creativite and build something beautiful and breath taking!
